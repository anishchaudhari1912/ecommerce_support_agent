Section 1: Disability & Accessibility Accommodations
1.1 Return Assistance
Customers with disabilities who are unable to package or ship returns independently:

May request a scheduled pickup at no additional cost
Must be requested through the accessibility support line or chat (mention disability accommodation)
Available in areas served by ShopCore's carrier partners (check eligibility by zip code)

1.2 Communication Accommodations
ShopCore provides:

TTY/relay service support via phone
Extended chat session times (no automatic timeout for accessibility chat sessions)
Screen-reader compatible documents on request
Large-print packing slips on request


Section 2: Bereavement & Life Events
2.1 Bereavement Policy
If a customer experiences a bereavement (death of a family member) and is unable to manage a return or dispute within the standard window:

Return window may be extended by up to 30 additional days (agent discretion, Tier 1)
Extension requires documentation or customer attestation (no documentation required for extensions under 14 days)

2.2 Natural Disaster / Emergency Circumstances
Customers affected by declared natural disasters or emergencies:

Return windows automatically extended by 60 days in affected zip codes (system-applied)
Expedited resolution for replacement of essential items (food, medicine, equipment)
Dedicated emergency support team activated during major disaster events


Section 3: Business / Wholesale Accounts
3.1 Business Account Return Policy
ShopCore Business accounts (accounts verified as businesses):

60-day return window on all eligible items
Dedicated account manager for disputes
Bulk return handling (palletized returns for large orders)
Must maintain business verification on file

3.2 Business Account Restrictions
Business accounts may not use:

Consumer promotion codes (unless specifically marked as applicable to business accounts)
A-to-Z Guarantee (business accounts have separate dispute resolution process)
Consumer cooling-off periods (statutory consumer rights may not apply to B2B transactions)


Section 4: Age-Restricted Items
4.1 Age Verification
Certain product categories require age verification:

Alcohol: 21+ verification required at checkout and delivery
Tobacco/nicotine products: 21+ verification required
Adult content products: 18+ verification required
Firearms accessories (legal items): State law compliance required

4.2 Returns on Age-Restricted Items

Alcohol: Non-refundable if opened; unopened may be returned within 7 days
Tobacco: Non-refundable once received (health and safety regulations)
Adult content: Subject to final sale terms; returns only for defective/damaged items


Section 5: Not-in-Policy Requests
5.1 Requests Not Covered by Policy
When a customer makes a request that is not addressed by any current policy:

Agent must not invent a policy or make up terms
Agent must not deny the request without escalating to verify
Agent should respond: "I'd like to make sure I address your situation correctly — let me escalate this to our specialist team"
Escalation to Tier 2 required for all not-in-policy decisions

5.2 Policy Gap Documentation
When agents identify a policy gap (a common scenario not covered by policy):

Document the gap via the internal Policy Gap Report form
Policy team reviews gaps weekly
Agents may NOT use precedent from other cases without documented policy backing

5.3 Customer Pressure for Policy Exceptions
If a customer applies pressure (threatening negative reviews, social media posts, legal action) to obtain outcomes outside policy:

Agents must not make exceptions due to customer pressure alone
Exceptions require legitimate policy basis or escalation approval
Agents should acknowledge frustration but not concede: "I understand this is frustrating. I want to help, but I'm unable to make exceptions outside of our policy without escalating this for you."
Document all pressure tactics in case notes


Section 6: International Customer Edge Cases
6.1 Import / Customs Issues
If an international order is delayed or refused at customs:

Refused by customs: Full refund issued; customer responsible for import duties/taxes paid
Delayed at customs: Not ShopCore's responsibility; standard carrier delay rules apply
Item seized by customs: Full refund issued; ShopCore will not reship seized items

6.2 Wrong Region Product (PAL/NTSC, voltage, etc.)
If a customer receives a product configured for the wrong region (e.g., wrong voltage adapter, region-locked media):

Treated as "significantly not as described" if region compatibility was not disclosed
Full refund or correct regional version if available

6.3 Translation Errors in Listings
If a listing was incorrectly translated and a customer purchased based on incorrect translated description:

Treated as "significantly not as described"
Full refund; customer keeps item if under $75
