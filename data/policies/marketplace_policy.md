Section 1: Seller Standards Overview
1.1 Seller Performance Metrics
Marketplace sellers must maintain:

Order defect rate: Below 1%
Pre-shipment cancellation rate: Below 2.5%
Late shipment rate: Below 4%
Valid tracking rate: Above 95%

Sellers failing to meet these standards may be suspended or removed from the marketplace.
1.2 Seller Response Time Requirements
Sellers are required to respond to customer messages within:

General inquiries: 24 hours
Return requests: 48 hours
Dispute/A-to-Z claims: 72 hours

Failure to respond within required windows allows ShopCore to make autonomous decisions on the dispute, typically in the customer's favor.
1.3 Product Listing Requirements
Sellers must ensure product listings are:

Accurate in description, specifications, and photos
Compliant with ShopCore's prohibited items policy
Priced without deceptive inflation for coupon/discount manipulation


Section 2: Seller Return Policies
2.1 Minimum Return Requirements
All marketplace sellers must offer at minimum:

14-day return window (ShopCore-mandated floor) for non-perishable goods
Free returns for items that are defective or not as described
Compliance with regional consumer protection laws in markets where they operate

2.2 Seller Extended Return Policies
Sellers may offer more generous return policies than the minimum:

Extended return windows (30, 60, 90 days)
Free returns for all orders
These enhanced terms are binding and displayed on the seller's storefront.

2.3 Seller Policy Exceptions
Sellers may apply stricter policies for:

Custom/made-to-order items: No returns (must be disclosed pre-purchase)
Perishable goods: Subject to same ShopCore perishable policy (POL-001)
International orders: Seller may require customer to pay return shipping
Hazardous materials: No returns (per regulation)


Section 3: ShopCore A-to-Z Guarantee (Detailed)
3.1 Coverage Scope
The A-to-Z Guarantee covers:

Package not received (after carrier investigation confirms loss)
Item significantly not as described (material differences from listing)
Item arrived damaged and seller does not resolve within 48 hours
Seller refuses return within their stated policy window
Seller becomes unresponsive or inactive

3.2 Coverage Exclusions
The A-to-Z Guarantee does NOT cover:

Remorse returns (buyer changed mind) — subject to seller's return policy only
Damage caused by customer after delivery
Buyer provided incorrect shipping address
Items that were correctly described but customer expected something different
Digital items once delivered/downloaded

3.3 A-to-Z Guarantee Process Timeline

Day 0: Customer contacts seller
Day 2 (minimum wait): If no seller response/resolution, customer can file A-to-Z claim
Day 2–5: ShopCore reviews claim, requests seller response if needed
Day 5–8: ShopCore decision issued
Day 8+: Refund processed if claim approved

3.4 A-to-Z Claim Limits

Maximum one A-to-Z claim per order
Claims must be filed within 90 days of estimated delivery date
Customers with excessive claims history (more than 5 in 12 months) may have claims reviewed with additional scrutiny


Section 4: First-Party vs. Marketplace Fulfillment
4.1 ShopCore Fulfilled Items
Items sold by ShopCore (first-party):

Full ShopCore policy applies (POL-001 through POL-008)
Handled by ShopCore customer service directly
Fastest resolution path

4.2 Fulfilled by ShopCore (FBS) — Seller-Listed Items
Items listed by marketplace sellers but stored/shipped by ShopCore:

ShopCore's shipping and delivery standards apply
Return policy: ShopCore's standard policy applies (overrides seller-specific policy for these items)
Disputes handled by ShopCore support directly

4.3 Seller-Fulfilled Items
Items listed AND shipped by marketplace sellers:

Seller's shipping timeline and methods apply
Seller's return policy applies (within ShopCore minimums)
Disputes first routed to seller; A-to-Z available as backstop


Section 5: Prohibited Seller Practices
5.1 Off-Platform Transactions
Sellers are prohibited from:

Directing customers to complete transactions off the ShopCore platform
Sharing personal contact information to facilitate off-platform purchases
Offering discounts contingent on off-platform payment

Violations are grounds for immediate seller suspension.
5.2 Counterfeit and Inauthentic Items
Sellers must not list counterfeit, replica, or inauthentic items. Confirmed counterfeit listings result in:

Immediate product removal
Seller account suspension
Potential legal referral
Customer full refund plus $25 inconvenience credit

5.3 Price Gouging
During declared emergencies or high-demand events, excessive price increases on essential goods are prohibited and may result in seller suspension.
