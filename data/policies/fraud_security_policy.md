Section 1: Unauthorized Transaction Policy
1.1 Reporting Unauthorized Charges
If a customer reports an unauthorized charge on their account:

Customer must report within 60 days of the statement date
Claim is reviewed by the Fraud Investigation Team
Account is temporarily frozen pending investigation
Investigation period: Up to 5 business days

1.2 Resolution of Unauthorized Charges
If unauthorized charge is confirmed:

Full refund to original payment method
Account password reset required
Two-factor authentication (2FA) strongly recommended
Fraudulent order cancelled and goods intercepted if possible

1.3 Customer Liability
Customers are not liable for unauthorized charges if:

The customer did not share account credentials
The account did not have an obvious/compromised password
The claim is made within 60 days
Customers may bear partial or full liability for unauthorized charges if account was compromised due to their own negligence (e.g., sharing passwords, using public devices without logging out).


Section 2: Phishing & Social Engineering
2.1 ShopCore Communications Policy
ShopCore will never:

Ask for account passwords via email, phone, or chat
Ask for full payment card numbers via any support channel
Send unsolicited requests for SSN or government ID

2.2 Suspicious Communication Reporting
If a customer receives a suspicious communication claiming to be ShopCore:

Do not click links or provide personal information
Forward the email to security@shopcore.example.com
Report via Account Security Center in account dashboard


Section 3: Account Takeover Prevention
3.1 Suspicious Login Activity
If suspicious login activity is detected:

Automated security hold placed on account
Customer notified via registered email and phone
Customer must verify identity before regaining access

3.2 Account Recovery
Account recovery options (in order of preference):

Email verification (registered email)
SMS verification (registered phone)
Identity verification via government ID (last resort, processed by Security team only)
Identity verification for account recovery may take up to 3 business days.


Section 4: Order Fraud Indicators
4.1 High-Risk Order Signals
Orders may be flagged for review if they exhibit:

Shipping address differs significantly from billing address
New account making high-value first purchase
Multiple different payment methods in quick succession
Expedited shipping to unusual location
Order patterns inconsistent with account history

4.2 Order Hold Process
Flagged orders are placed on hold and customer is contacted:

Customer must verify order via email confirmation link
High-value flagged orders may require phone verification
If unverified within 24 hours: Order cancelled and full refund issued


Section 5: Data Protection & Privacy
5.1 Data Agents Can Access
Support agents are authorized to access:

Order details (order number, items, dates, amounts)
Shipping address
Contact information
Account status and order history

5.2 Sensitive Data Agents Cannot Share
Agents must never share or confirm:

Full payment card numbers (last 4 only may be confirmed)
CVV numbers
Full Social Security Numbers
Passwords or security question answers
Data belonging to a different customer account

5.3 Verification Before Account Actions
Before making any account changes or processing refunds, agents must verify customer identity by confirming at least two of:

Email address on file
Order number
Last 4 digits of payment method
Shipping address
Phone number on file
