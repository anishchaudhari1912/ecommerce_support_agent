Section 1: Incorrect Item Received
1.1 Wrong Item Delivered
If a customer receives a different item than what was ordered:

Full refund OR replacement of correct item (customer's choice)
Customer does not need to return the incorrect item if its value is under $50
For items over $50: Prepaid return label provided; refund/replacement issued upon return
Report must be made within 30 days of delivery

1.2 Partial Order Missing Items
If an order arrives with missing items:

Missing items reshipped at no charge, OR refund for missing items' value
Must be reported within 14 days of delivery
Large orders (10+ items) may require additional verification

1.3 Wrong Variant (Color, Size, etc.)
If the correct product was delivered but in the wrong variant (e.g., wrong color or size):

Treated as an incorrect item (see 1.1)
Exchange for correct variant preferred; refund also available


Section 2: Damaged Item Received
2.1 Physical Damage on Arrival
For items damaged during shipping or at the warehouse:

Photo/video evidence required for claims over $30
Full refund OR replacement (customer's choice)
Customer does not need to return damaged item
Report within 48 hours of delivery for shipping damage; within 7 days for manufacturing defects discovered on arrival

2.2 Cosmetic vs. Functional Damage

Cosmetic damage (scratches, dents not affecting function): Partial refund of 10–30% at agent discretion, or return for full refund
Functional damage (item does not work): Full refund or replacement
Agents must distinguish between customer-caused damage and arrival damage

2.3 Electronics Damage
For electronics:

Dead-on-arrival (DOA): Full refund or replacement regardless of price
Physical damage suggesting misuse: Not covered; warranty may apply separately
Manufacturer warranty claims: Directed to manufacturer; ShopCore may facilitate


Section 3: Item Not as Described
3.1 Significantly Not as Described (SNAD)
A claim of "significantly not as described" is valid when:

The item materially differs from the product listing (e.g., different material, missing key feature)
Photos on listing were misleading or inaccurate
Product specifications on listing were incorrect

Resolution: Full refund including original shipping; customer may keep item if under $75; over $75 requires return.
3.2 Minor Discrepancies
Minor differences (slight color variation due to screen calibration, minor size variation within tolerance) do not qualify as SNAD. These are treated as standard return requests subject to normal return policy.
3.3 Counterfeit / Authenticity Disputes
If a customer suspects they received a counterfeit item:

Escalate immediately to Fraud & Authenticity team
Do not process standard refund until investigation completes
Customer should preserve item and packaging
Investigation period: Up to 10 business days
If counterfeit confirmed: Full refund + $25 inconvenience credit; seller suspended


Section 4: Marketplace Dispute Resolution
4.1 A-to-Z Guarantee Process
For marketplace orders, the A-to-Z Guarantee protects customers from:

Item not received
Item significantly not as described
Seller refuses legitimate return within policy window
Item arrived damaged and seller is unresponsive

4.2 A-to-Z Claim Process

Customer must contact seller first and wait 48 hours for response
If unresolved, customer opens A-to-Z claim via account dashboard
ShopCore reviews within 3 business days
If claim approved: ShopCore refunds customer and seeks reimbursement from seller

4.3 A-to-Z Claim Denials
Claims may be denied if:

Customer did not contact seller first
Claim is for a remorse return (not a seller failure)
Customer provided incorrect delivery address
Item was returned and seller issued refund already


Section 5: Chargeback & Payment Disputes
5.1 Chargeback Process
If a customer files a chargeback with their payment provider:

ShopCore will respond to the chargeback with order evidence
Customers who file chargebacks may have orders cancelled and accounts reviewed
Customers are encouraged to resolve disputes with ShopCore directly before filing chargebacks

5.2 Friendly Fraud Prevention
Accounts with a history of excessive chargebacks or return fraud may be flagged for review. Actions may include:

Reduced return approvals
Requirement for return before refund (no immediate refund)
Account suspension in severe cases


Section 6: Missing Delivery Dispute (Marked Delivered)
6.1 Delivered But Not Received
If tracking shows "delivered" but customer did not receive the package:

48-hour waiting period required (item may have been left with neighbor or misdelivered)
Customer must check common delivery spots (porch, mailroom, with building management)
After 48 hours: ShopCore initiates carrier investigation
Carrier investigation: Up to 5 business days

6.2 Resolution After Investigation

If carrier confirms mis-delivery or loss: Full refund or replacement
If carrier confirms delivery to correct address: Case-by-case review; police report may be required for claims over $150
First-time claim without suspicious history: Courtesy resolution (refund or replacement) may be extended


Section 7: Return Fraud Prevention
7.1 Wardrobing / Return Abuse
Returning items after use without disclosure is considered return fraud. Indicators include:

Worn clothing returned as new
Electronics returned with components missing
Consumables returned partially used

ShopCore reserves the right to deny returns, deduct restocking fees (up to 20%), or suspend accounts for return fraud.
7.2 High-Return Rate Accounts
Accounts with a return rate exceeding 30% of orders over a 12-month period may:

Have returns reviewed individually
Be required to return items before refund (no advance refund)
Receive reduced future purchase limits
