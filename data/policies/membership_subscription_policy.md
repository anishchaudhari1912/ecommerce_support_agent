Section 1: ShopCore Plus Membership
1.1 Membership Tiers
ShopCore Plus offers two tiers:

Plus Standard: $9.99/month or $89/year

Free standard shipping on all orders
2x loyalty points on ShopCore-fulfilled orders
Early sale access (24 hours)


Plus Prime: $14.99/month or $129/year

All Plus Standard benefits
Free expedited (2-day) shipping on orders over $25
3x loyalty points on all orders
Dedicated support line (48-hour max response guarantee)
Free extended returns (60 days instead of 30)



1.2 Membership Billing

Monthly memberships billed on subscription anniversary date
Annual memberships billed on annual anniversary date
Free trial period: 30 days for new members (credit card required)

1.3 Membership Cancellation & Refunds

Monthly membership: Cancel anytime; no refund for current month; access continues until period end
Annual membership — within 30 days of purchase: Pro-rated refund for unused months
Annual membership — after 30 days: No refund; access continues until end date
Free trial cancellation: Cancel before day 30 to avoid charge (cancel by day 29 to be safe)


Section 2: ShopCore Plus Return Policy Differences
2.1 Extended Return Window (Plus Prime)
Plus Prime members benefit from a 60-day return window instead of the standard 30-day window:

Applies to eligible items only (same category restrictions as standard policy)
Does not extend windows for final sale, perishables, or other non-returnable categories
Return window measured from delivery date

2.2 Free Return Shipping for Plus Members

Plus Standard: Free returns on apparel, shoes, accessories (same as standard)
Plus Prime: Free returns on all eligible categories (no customer-paid returns on eligible items)

2.3 Plus Prime Dedicated Support

Estimated response time: Maximum 48 hours
Priority queue: Plus Prime tickets handled before standard tickets
Dedicated phone support line available Mon–Fri 8AM–8PM ET


Section 3: Subscription Box Services
3.1 Subscription Box Orders
ShopCore offers curated subscription boxes in categories including:

Beauty & Skincare (monthly)
Gourmet Food & Snacks (monthly/quarterly)
Fitness & Wellness (monthly)
Book Club (monthly)

3.2 Subscription Box Return Policy

Subscription box items may be returned individually under standard return policy
Full box return: Must be returned within 14 days; all items must be included
Perishable items in subscription boxes: Non-refundable unless damaged or spoiled on arrival
Complaint about box curation (didn't like items): Not a valid refund reason; shoppable alternatives may be offered

3.3 Subscription Box Skipping & Pausing

Members may skip one box per 12 months per subscription
Pausing: Available for up to 3 months per calendar year
Pause must be set at least 5 days before next billing date


Section 4: Gift Memberships
4.1 Gift Membership Terms
ShopCore Plus memberships may be purchased as gifts:

Available in 3-month, 6-month, and 12-month gift increments
Non-refundable once redeemed
Unredeemed gift memberships refundable within 60 days of purchase if not activated

4.2 Gift Membership + Existing Membership
If a recipient already has an active Plus membership:

Gift duration is added to existing membership expiration
Membership tier is upgraded to gifted tier if higher
Downgrade does not occur (if recipient has higher tier than gifted)
