Section 1: General Return Policy
1.1 Standard Return Window
Customers may return most items within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund to the original payment method. Items must be:

Unused and in original condition
In original packaging with all tags attached
Accompanied by proof of purchase (order number or receipt)

1.2 Extended Return Window
During the holiday season (November 1 – December 31), items purchased may be returned until January 31 of the following year. This extension applies to standard eligible items only.
1.3 Return Shipping

Free returns: Apparel, shoes, and accessories qualify for free return shipping via prepaid label.
Customer-paid returns: Electronics, large items, and furniture — customer pays return shipping unless item is defective or incorrect.
Return shipping cost: $6.99 flat rate for customer-paid returns using our label; customer may use own carrier.


Section 2: Refund Processing
2.1 Refund Timeline

Credit/debit card refunds: 3–5 business days after return is received and inspected.
Store credit: Immediate upon return approval.
PayPal/digital wallets: 1–3 business days.
Gift card refunds: Returned as store credit only.

2.2 Partial Refunds
Partial refunds (up to 50% of item value) may be issued at agent discretion when:

Item is returned without original packaging
Item shows minor signs of use but is otherwise functional
Item is missing accessories or components (deduction applied per missing piece)

2.3 Refund Method
Refunds are always issued to the original payment method. If the original payment method is no longer valid (e.g., expired card), store credit is issued instead.

Section 3: Non-Returnable / Final Sale Items
3.1 Categories Ineligible for Return
The following categories are non-returnable under any circumstances:

Perishable goods: Food, beverages, flowers, plants, fresh produce
Hygiene items: Underwear, swimwear (if tried on without liner), razors, electric shavers (if used), personal care devices after use
Digital products: Software licenses, digital downloads, gift cards (once activated)
Hazardous materials: Batteries, chemicals, flammable items
Custom/personalized items: Engraved, monogrammed, or made-to-order items

3.2 Final Sale Items
Items marked "Final Sale" at time of purchase are non-returnable and non-refundable. Final Sale status is displayed on the product page and confirmed at checkout. No exceptions apply.
3.3 Opened Consumables
Opened consumable items (supplements, skincare products, cosmetics) may only be returned if:

The product caused an adverse reaction (medical documentation may be required for large refunds over $100)
The product was received damaged or tampered with


Section 4: Defective / Damaged Items
4.1 Defective Item Policy
If an item is received defective or stops functioning within the warranty period, customers are entitled to:

Full replacement at no charge (subject to availability)
Full refund including original shipping cost
Repair service (for applicable electronics/appliances)

Defective items do not need to be returned for refunds under $75. For items over $75, return of the defective item is required.
4.2 Damaged in Transit
If an item arrives damaged due to shipping:

Report within 48 hours of delivery with photo evidence
Full refund or replacement issued at customer's choice
Customer does not need to return damaged item (item may be kept)
ShopCore files a carrier claim internally

4.3 Evidence Requirements
For damage/defect claims:

Photo or video evidence required for claims over $50
Description of defect required for all claims
Original packaging photo required if damage is suspected to be transit-related


Section 5: Marketplace Seller Returns
5.1 Seller-Fulfilled Orders
For items sold and fulfilled by third-party marketplace sellers:

Seller's individual return policy applies and may differ from ShopCore's standard policy
Seller return window: Minimum 14 days (ShopCore-mandated floor); sellers may offer more
ShopCore is not responsible for seller policy differences but provides mediation

5.2 ShopCore Guarantee
If a marketplace seller refuses a legitimate return within their stated policy window, ShopCore's A-to-Z Guarantee applies:

Customer must first attempt to resolve with seller (minimum 48-hour wait)
ShopCore may issue a courtesy refund and pursue reimbursement from seller
A-to-Z Guarantee covers items not received, significantly not as described, or arrived damaged

5.3 Seller Return Restrictions
Marketplace sellers may set stricter return policies for:

Custom items made to order
International orders
Items with special handling requirements
These restrictions must be disclosed on the product listing page.


Section 6: Perishable & Food Item Policy
6.1 Non-Refundable Perishables
Perishable food items are generally non-refundable once delivered, with exceptions for:

Items arrived spoiled, damaged, or not as described
Items delivered significantly outside promised temperature window (for cold-chain items)
Items with incorrect or missing allergen labeling

6.2 Perishable Damage Claims
For perishable damage claims:

Must be reported within 24 hours of delivery
Photo evidence of spoilage/damage required
Customer may keep the item; refund or replacement issued
Full refund issued without requiring return

6.3 Food Quality Disputes
"I don't like the taste" or subjective quality complaints for perishables do not qualify for refunds. Objective quality failures (mold, incorrect item, missing items) do qualify.

Section 7: High-Value Item Returns
7.1 Items Over $500
For items valued over $500:

Return must be initiated within 14 days (not 30) unless item is defective
Inspection at ShopCore warehouse required before refund is processed
Agent manager approval required for refund processing
Identity verification may be required

7.2 Luxury and Jewelry
Fine jewelry and luxury goods:

Must include original certificates of authenticity
Must be unworn and in original condition
14-day return window
Refund issued only after authentication check (up to 5 business days additional)
