Section 1: Accepted Payment Methods
1.1 Standard Payment Methods
ShopCore accepts the following payment methods:

Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover (credit and debit)
PayPal, PayPal Pay Later
Apple Pay, Google Pay
ShopCore Gift Cards
ShopCore Store Credit
Bank transfer (ACH) — orders over $500 only

1.2 Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL)
ShopCore partners with Affirm and Afterpay for BNPL:

Affirm: 3, 6, or 12-month payment plans; interest-free promotional options
Afterpay: 4 biweekly payments, interest-free
BNPL eligibility is determined by the BNPL provider, not ShopCore
Refunds on BNPL orders: Refunded to BNPL account; BNPL provider manages remaining installment plan

1.3 Gift Cards

ShopCore gift cards are redeemable for any ShopCore purchase
Gift cards cannot be refunded or exchanged for cash (except as required by law)
California state law: Gift cards with balance under $10 can be redeemed for cash
Gift card balance is non-expiring
Lost/stolen gift cards can be replaced with original proof of purchase


Section 2: Billing & Charges
2.1 When Charges Occur

Standard orders: Charged at time of shipment (not at order placement)
Pre-orders: Charged at time of shipment (unless customer selects early charge option)
Subscriptions: Charged on billing cycle date
Digital products: Charged immediately upon purchase

2.2 Failed Payments
If a payment fails after order placement:

Customer notified immediately via email
24-hour window to update payment method before order is cancelled
After 24 hours with no update: Order cancelled; items return to inventory

2.3 Currency
ShopCore operates primarily in USD for domestic US orders. International orders are charged in USD or local currency as selected at checkout. Exchange rates are determined at time of charge.

Section 3: Refunds to Payment Methods
3.1 Credit/Debit Card Refunds

Processed within 1 business day by ShopCore
Appears on statement within 3–5 business days (bank-dependent)
Refund goes to card used at purchase only

3.2 PayPal Refunds

Processed within 1 business day by ShopCore
Credited to PayPal balance within 1–3 business days

3.3 BNPL Refunds

Affirm: Refund credited to Affirm account; Affirm adjusts remaining payments
Afterpay: Refund credited to Afterpay account; refund may take up to 10 business days to process
If all installments have been paid: Refund returned to original debit/credit card on file with BNPL provider

3.4 Store Credit Refunds
Store credit is issued:

Immediately upon approval
Appears in account wallet within minutes
No expiration date


Section 4: Disputed Charges & Billing Errors
4.1 Billing Error Process
If a customer believes they were incorrectly charged:

Contact ShopCore support within 60 days of the charge
Provide order number and description of discrepancy
ShopCore will review and respond within 3 business days

4.2 Duplicate Charge Resolution
Duplicate charges (same order charged twice):

Full refund of duplicate charge processed immediately upon verification
Confirmation email sent to customer
Refund appears within 3–5 business days

4.3 Overcharge Resolution
If customer was charged more than the order total:

Difference refunded within 3–5 business days
$5 store credit issued as inconvenience compensation
