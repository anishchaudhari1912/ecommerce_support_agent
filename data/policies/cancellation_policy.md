Section 1: Cancellation Windows
1.1 Standard Cancellation Window
Orders may be cancelled free of charge within 1 hour of placement, provided the order has not yet moved to "Processing" status. After 1 hour, cancellations are subject to the conditions below.
1.2 Pre-Shipment Cancellation
If the order is in "Processing" status but has not yet shipped:

Cancellation request may be submitted but is not guaranteed
ShopCore will attempt to intercept the order
If interception is successful: Full refund within 3–5 business days
If interception fails: Order must be returned after delivery under the standard return policy

1.3 Post-Shipment Cancellation
Once an order has shipped (tracking number assigned), it cannot be cancelled. Customer must:

Wait for delivery
Initiate a return after delivery
Or refuse delivery (item returned to sender; refund issued upon receipt at warehouse)


Section 2: Non-Cancellable Orders
2.1 Instant-Fulfillment Orders
The following order types cannot be cancelled after placement:

Digital downloads (delivered immediately)
Gift card purchases
Custom/personalized items (production begins immediately)
Food & perishable subscription orders (for current billing cycle)

2.2 Flash Sale Orders
Orders placed during flash sales or limited-time promotions may have restricted cancellation windows (as short as 15 minutes) due to inventory reservation systems. Promotion terms will specify any cancellation restrictions.
2.3 Pre-Orders
Pre-orders may be cancelled any time before the item ships. Once shipped, standard post-shipment rules apply.

Section 3: Subscription Cancellations
3.1 Subscription Service Cancellation
Subscription services (monthly boxes, replenishment programs) may be cancelled at any time, but:

Cancellation takes effect at the next billing cycle
Current billing period is non-refundable unless service has not been rendered
Partial-month refunds are not issued for subscription services

3.2 Free Trial Cancellation
Free trials must be cancelled before the trial end date to avoid charges. No refund is issued for charges incurred due to failure to cancel before trial expiration.
3.3 Annual Subscription Refunds
Annual subscriptions cancelled within 30 days of purchase: Pro-rated refund for unused months.
Annual subscriptions cancelled after 30 days: No refund; access continues until subscription end date.

Section 4: Marketplace Seller Cancellations
4.1 Seller-Fulfilled Cancellations
Cancellation policies for marketplace seller orders vary by seller:

Seller must honor cancellations made within 30 minutes of order placement
Sellers may set their own cancellation terms beyond this window
ShopCore requires sellers to display cancellation terms on their storefront

4.2 Seller Cancellation Initiated
If a seller cancels an order:

Full refund issued automatically within 3–5 business days
Customer is notified by email within 2 hours
$5 ShopCore coupon issued as compensation for the inconvenience


Section 5: Cancellation Refund Method
5.1 Pre-Shipment Cancellation Refunds

Refunded to original payment method within 3–5 business days
Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL) plans: Cancellation may take up to 7 business days to reflect

5.2 Post-Return Processing
For cancellations that required return of item: Refund processed within 5–7 business days after warehouse receives the item.
