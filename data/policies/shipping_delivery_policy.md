Section 1: Shipping Options & Timelines
1.1 Standard Shipping

Estimated delivery: 5–7 business days from shipment date
Available for all domestic US orders
Free for orders over $35; $4.99 for orders under $35

1.2 Expedited Shipping

Estimated delivery: 2–3 business days from shipment date
Cost: $9.99 flat rate or free for Prime/Plus members
Not available for oversized or hazardous items

1.3 Overnight Shipping

Estimated delivery: Next business day if ordered before 1 PM local time
Cost: $19.99
Not available for marketplace seller items, oversized items, or certain regions

1.4 International Shipping

Available to 45+ countries
Estimated delivery: 7–21 business days depending on destination
Import duties and taxes: Customer's responsibility (not included in ShopCore price)
Some items cannot be shipped internationally (restricted by category or regulation)


Section 2: Delivery Guarantees
2.1 Estimated vs. Guaranteed Delivery

Standard and expedited shipping dates are estimates, not guarantees
Overnight shipping dates are guaranteed for eligible orders placed before the cutoff time
Weather, carrier delays, and force majeure events void delivery date guarantees

2.2 Overnight Guarantee Failure
If overnight shipping fails to deliver on the guaranteed date (excluding force majeure):

Full shipping fee refunded automatically
$10 store credit issued as compensation
Applies only to ShopCore-fulfilled orders

2.3 Late Delivery Compensation
For standard or expedited orders arriving more than 5 business days after estimated date:

$5 store credit issued upon request
Does not apply during peak periods (Nov 15 – Jan 5) or force majeure events


Section 3: Lost Packages
3.1 Definition of Lost
A package is considered lost if:

Tracking shows no update for 5+ business days (domestic)
Tracking shows no update for 10+ business days (international)
Carrier confirms loss in writing
Package was marked "delivered" but customer reports non-receipt

3.2 Lost Package Investigation
Before a lost package claim is processed:

Customer must confirm delivery address is correct
48-hour waiting period required after "delivered" status (for packages that may be misdelivered locally)
ShopCore initiates a carrier trace investigation
Carrier investigation may take up to 5 business days

3.3 Lost Package Resolution
Upon confirmation of loss:

Full refund to original payment method, OR
Replacement shipment (if item is in stock)
Customer's choice of resolution

3.4 Porch Piracy / Stolen After Delivery
If tracking confirms delivery but customer reports theft:

ShopCore will review case-by-case
One-time courtesy refund/replacement for verified customers with no prior theft claims
Police report may be requested for claims over $150
Subsequent theft claims require police report and may be denied


Section 4: Incorrect Address & Failed Delivery
4.1 Customer-Provided Wrong Address
If the package cannot be delivered due to an incorrect address provided by the customer:

ShopCore is not responsible for the loss
If package is returned to warehouse: Reshipped at customer expense ($9.99) or refund issued minus original shipping cost
If package is not returned: No refund issued; customer must repurchase

4.2 Failed Delivery Attempts

Carriers make up to 3 delivery attempts
After 3 failed attempts, package held at carrier facility for 5 business days
If not picked up, package returned to ShopCore
Re-delivery: Customer pays re-delivery fee ($5.99)


Section 5: Regional Shipping Restrictions
5.1 US Territories
Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico, Guam, US Virgin Islands:

Standard shipping: 7–14 business days
Overnight and 2-day shipping not available
Additional shipping surcharges may apply

5.2 PO Boxes and Military Addresses (APO/FPO/DPO)

Standard shipping only (no expedited options)
Delivery timeline: 7–21 business days
Not all items available for PO Box / military address delivery

5.3 International Shipping Restrictions
Some items cannot be shipped internationally:

Hazardous materials (batteries over certain capacity, chemicals)
Items restricted by destination country regulations
Oversized/overweight items
Live plants and certain food products


Section 6: Shipping During Promotions and Peak Periods
6.1 Peak Period Delays
During peak periods (Black Friday week, Christmas week, major sale events):

Shipping estimates may be extended by 2–4 business days
Delivery guarantees may be suspended
Customers are notified during checkout of any active delays

6.2 Free Shipping Thresholds

Standard free shipping: Orders over $35
Plus/Prime members: Free standard shipping on all orders
Free expedited shipping: Orders over $125 (Plus/Prime only)
