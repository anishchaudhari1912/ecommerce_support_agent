Section 1: Coupon & Promo Code Terms
1.1 General Coupon Rules

One coupon/promo code per order unless otherwise stated
Coupons cannot be combined with other coupons unless explicitly permitted
Coupons apply to the subtotal before tax and shipping
Minimum purchase requirements apply where specified on the coupon

1.2 Coupon Expiration

Coupons expire on the date listed; expired coupons cannot be honored
No extensions are granted for expired coupons
Exception: System errors that prevented a valid coupon from applying may be reviewed

1.3 Coupon Stackability
Some promotions are flagged as "stackable" and may be combined:

Site-wide percentage discounts (e.g., 20% off) may stack with product-specific coupons only if both coupons are marked stackable
Loyalty points redemption can always be combined with one coupon
Free shipping coupons can always be combined with product discount coupons

1.4 Coupon Restrictions
Coupons may be restricted to:

Specific product categories
Minimum order values
First-time purchases only
Specific customer accounts
ShopCore-fulfilled items only (not marketplace sellers)
Non-sale items only


Section 2: Price Match Policy
2.1 Price Match Eligibility
ShopCore will match a competitor's advertised price if:

The item is identical (same model, size, color)
The competitor is a major retailer (list maintained internally)
The competitor item is in stock at time of request
Price match request is made within 7 days of purchase

2.2 Price Match Exclusions
Price match does not apply to:

Marketplace seller prices (third-party)
Flash sales, limited-time deals, or doorbuster prices
Membership/loyalty pricing requiring competitor membership
Prices that include rebates or mail-in offers
Refurbished, used, or open-box items

2.3 Price Drop on ShopCore
If the price of a ShopCore item drops within 48 hours of purchase:

Customer may request a price adjustment (one per order)
Difference refunded as store credit
Does not apply to flash sale prices or items on clearance


Section 3: Loyalty Points & Rewards
3.1 Points Earning

Standard: 1 point per $1 spent on ShopCore-fulfilled items
Plus/Prime members: 2 points per $1
Marketplace purchases: 0.5 points per $1
Points are awarded after order is delivered and return window closes

3.2 Points Redemption

100 points = $1 discount
Minimum redemption: 500 points ($5)
Maximum redemption per order: 10,000 points ($100)
Points cannot be redeemed for gift cards or cash

3.3 Points Expiration

Points expire after 12 months of account inactivity
Purchase activity resets the 12-month clock for all existing points
Points earned on returned orders are deducted upon return processing


Section 4: Referral & Affiliate Programs
4.1 Referral Bonuses

Referrer receives $10 store credit when referred customer makes their first purchase of $25+
Referred customer receives $10 off first order of $25+
Referral credits applied within 30 days of qualifying purchase
Maximum referral credits: $200 per calendar year per account

4.2 Affiliate Restrictions
Affiliate links and coupon codes may not be used on the affiliate's own purchases. Self-referral is strictly prohibited and will result in reversal of any credits and potential account suspension.

Section 5: Sale Events & Clearance
5.1 Sale Price Validity
Sale prices are valid only during the sale event period. ShopCore does not extend sale pricing after the event ends, except for checkout errors documented at time of purchase.
5.2 Clearance Items
Clearance items are sold at reduced prices and may have limited return options:

Clearance items under $20: Final sale (no returns)
Clearance items $20 and over: Standard 30-day return policy applies
Clearance items marked "Final Sale": No returns under any circumstances

5.3 Buy One Get One (BOGO) Promotions
For BOGO promotions:

If the paid item is returned: Full price charged for the "free" item (coupon reversed)
If the free item is returned: No refund issued for the free item
Both items must be returned to receive a full refund on a BOGO purchase


Section 6: Promotional Abuse Prevention
6.1 Prohibited Conduct
ShopCore reserves the right to cancel orders and revoke promotional benefits if:

Multiple accounts are used to claim single-use promotions
Promotions are used for resale purposes
Fraudulent returns are combined with promotional purchases

6.2 Enforcement
Accounts engaged in promotional abuse may be subject to:

Coupon/promo code revocation
Account suspension
Order cancellation without refund for fraudulent activity
